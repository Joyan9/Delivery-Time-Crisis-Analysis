## North Star Metric
What is the single most important metric for this specific problem?

- **Average Delivery Time**
- Justification - Since this metric signifies whether the company is able to keep up the promise of 10 minutes delivery, I choose to use this as my One Metric That Matters (OMTM).

## Secondary Metrics
What other supporting metrics will help you diagnose the root cause?

1. **Orders to Active Riders Ratio Per Store / Zone** - To understand if the issue is limited to a few areas or widespread.
2. **Cancellation Rate - To quantify impact of lost revenue**
